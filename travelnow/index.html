Under the hood

No black boxes. The globe, the projection, the clipping and the visa logic are all readable vanilla JavaScript.

  • Orthographic projection computed per-frame, with great-circle horizon clipping and limb arcs so polygons wrap the sphere cleanly.
  • 39,402 visa rules from the open passport-index dataset, bundled as compact JSON.
  • Natural Earth geometry (ISO-A3), trimmed for a tiny payload.
  • Service worker caches the shell and data for instant, offline loads.
